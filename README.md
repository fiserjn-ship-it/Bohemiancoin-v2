## The Bohemiancoin Manifesto

### Vision: Order, Responsibility, and True Freedom
Bohemiancoin v2 is built on the principle of **mathematical justice**. Unlike traditional systems that favor capital concentration, we prioritize the individual user. Our philosophy is simple: absolute control over your assets comes with absolute responsibility for your data.

### Technical Architecture
* **Fixed-State Ledger (The Codex):** We abandon the concept of infinitely growing chains. The network state is anchored in a fixed **512MB Codex**, acting as a high-performance State Database.
* **The Cubic Seal (Consensus):** A geometric approach to validation. By tying network security to physical disk-reading limits and 3D navigation, we eliminate the dominance of centralized server farms.
* **CBOR Receipts:** Transaction history lives in your pocket, not on a cloud. Users hold cryptographic proofs (Merkle Proofs) of their wealth, enabling secure offline verification.

### Governance & Fairness
We implement the **15:10:5 Rule** for transaction processing. This ensures that the network remains accessible to everyone—balancing priority seekers with patient, honest participants. No user is ignored by the protocol.
