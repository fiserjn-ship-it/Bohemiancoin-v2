# BOHEMIANCOIN [cite: 1]

Today's financial systems, and unfortunately many cryptocurrencies as well, often become opaque, bloat to giant proportions, and favor those with the greatest capital [cite: 2]. **Bohemiancoin v2** was born from the need to change this direction [cite: 3]. It returns control to the ordinary user, but with a clear message: true freedom requires firm order and personal responsibility [cite: 3].

The system does not promise effortless miracles, but **mathematical justice** [cite: 4]. Instead of endless data growth, Bohemiancoin is anchored in a fixed, **512MB Codex** [cite: 4]. You don't need giant data centers to participate in the network's operation [cite: 5].

### Key Pillars of the System
* Your digital ownership does not reside on third-party servers, but exclusively in your pocket in the form of CBOR receipts [cite: 6].
* Thanks to this, you can safely store your assets even in a completely offline environment and cryptographically prove their existence and origin to anyone [cite: 7].
* With this absolute control comes the responsibility for your own data—the history of the network lives in the pockets of its users, not in the cloud [cite: 8].

### Network Mechanics
* The competitive struggle of Hunters (miners) changes from mindless electricity burning to elegant, geometric precision thanks to The Cubic Seal algorithm [cite: 9].
* And thanks to the 15:10:5 transaction rule, it is ensured that the network ignores no one [cite: 10].
* Those in a hurry can pay extra, but the system will always mandatorily process transactions from those who wait honestly and patiently in line [cite: 11].

Bohemiancoin is a tool for those who want to have their finances under 100% control, respect immutable rules, and are willing to accept the responsibility that comes with true independence [cite: 12].

* The system does not promise effortless wealth [cite: 18].
* It promises firm rules applicable to everyone equally [cite: 18].
* Transactions are processed according to the **15:10:5** rule [cite: 18]:
    * **15 slots — Market Priority:** Fifteen spots for those who are in a hurry and pay extra [cite: 19].
    * **10 slots — Social Peace:** Ten spots for those waiting patiently in line. Guaranteed access [cite: 20].
    * **5 slots — Network Infrastructure:** Five spots for the management and development of the network itself [cite: 21].
* Coin emission is fixed and finite: **42,000,000 Groschen** (GRS) in three epochs of four years [cite: 22].
* Each block releases exactly **6.6 GRS** [cite: 23].
* No inflation out of thin air [cite: 23].

* The competitive struggle for block validation — **Cubic Seal** — is not a race in burning electricity [cite: 25].
* It is geometric precision: each validator searches for its own target in a three-dimensional space, defined by its identity, network time, and transaction content [cite: 26].
* The result depends on the physical disk, not the power of cartels [cite: 27].
* Farms have no advantage [cite: 27].
* Cloud storage is disadvantaged [cite: 27].
* The winner is the one who has real hardware and a real interest in the network [cite: 28].
